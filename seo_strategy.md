# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing landing page (`/` when unauthenticated)
- Public pricing page (`/pricing`)
- Public authentication entry pages (`/login`, `/signup`, `/forgot-password`, `/reset-password`, `/login-otp`) only where they affect crawlability, indexing, or shared metadata

## Out of scope
- Authenticated application routes (`/discover`, `/bounties/**`, `/submissions`, `/earnings`, `/profile`, `/settings`, `/referral`, `/launchpad`, `/admin`)
- Internal/admin product workflows after login

## Target audience
- Creators looking for paid crypto/Web3 bounty opportunities
- Users who want AI-assisted research briefs, scoring, and production plans for bounties

## Primary keywords
- crypto bounties
- web3 bounties
- creator bounties
- AI bounty scoring
- creator income from bounties
